6Cookies & Tracking Technologies

6.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience and understand how you use our site.

6.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for website to function (shopping cart, login) Session
Performance Cookies Analyze how visitors use the site (Google Analytics) Up to 2 years
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences (language, location) Up to 1 year
Marketing Cookies Track visits across websites for advertising Up to 1 year

6.3 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • Social Media: Facebook, Instagram pixels (if enabled)
  • Advertising: Retargeting ads (if used)

6.4 Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Opt-Out Links:
    • Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Do Not Track: We respect browser DNT signals
Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. You may not be able to place orders or use certain features.

9Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Rights Available to All Customers

Right to Access

You can request:

  • Copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Details about how we use your information
  • Who we've shared your information with

Right to Correction

You can request that we:

  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Update outdated information
  • Complete incomplete information

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your information when:

  • It's no longer needed for original purpose
  • You withdraw consent (for consent-based processing)
  • You object to processing
  • It was unlawfully processed

Note: We may retain information required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Right to Opt-Out of Marketing

You can opt-out of marketing communications:

  • Click "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Reply "STOP" to marketing text messages
  • Contact us directly to opt-out
  • Update preferences in your account settings

Note: You'll still receive transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates).

Right to Data Portability

You can request:

  • Your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Transfer of your data to another service (where technically feasible)

9.2 Additional Rights (GDPR - EU/UK Residents)

If you're in the EU or UK, you also have:

Right to Object

  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Object to direct marketing at any time
  • Object to automated decision-making

Right to Restrict Processing

Request restriction when:

  • You contest accuracy of the data
  • Processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion
  • We no longer need data but you need it for legal claims
  • You've objected to processing pending verification

Right to Lodge a Complaint

  • File complaint with your local data protection authority
  • We'd appreciate opportunity to resolve issues first

9.3 Additional Rights (CCPA - California Residents)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

9.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  1. Contact us via email: privacy@avilafarmusa.com
  2. Or call us at: [Your Phone Number]
  3. Provide your name and email/phone used with us
  4. Specify which right you wish to exercise
  5. We'll verify your identity and respond within 30 days
No Charge: Exercising your privacy rights is free. We may charge a reasonable fee for excessive, repetitive, or clearly unfounded requests.
