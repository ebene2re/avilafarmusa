  • All birds must arrive alive and in good health

Call or text our direct line as soon as you notice any issue. For DOA claims, contact within 2 hours of delivery. For health issues, contact within 24 hours of noticing symptoms.

Care Requirements for Guarantee Validity

To maintain guarantee coverage, customers must follow these essential care protocols:

Quarantine Protocol (CRITICAL)

  • Minimum 30-day quarantine from existing birds
  • Separate housing, feeding, and watering systems
  • No shared equipment or tools
  • Monitor for any signs of illness daily
  • Consult veterinarian if any concerns arise

Housing Requirements

  • Minimum 4 sq ft per bird indoor space
  • 8-10 sq ft per bird outdoor run space
  • Proper ventilation without drafts
  • Clean, dry bedding changed regularly
  • Protection from predators and extreme weather

Nutrition Requirements

  • Follow our provided feeding guidelines
  • High-quality game bird feed (20-24% protein)
  • Fresh, clean water available 24/7
  • Appropriate supplements as recommended
  • No sudden diet changes

Health Monitoring

  • Daily observation for behavioral changes
  • Weekly weight checks
  • Monthly health assessments
  • Prompt veterinary care if needed
  • Maintain health records

Frequently Asked Questions

What if a bird dies during shipping?

This is extremely rare (less than 1% with our carriers), but if it happens, we replace the bird immediately at no cost. You must report it within 2 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. We've never had a customer pay for a DOA bird in 37 years.

Can I get a refund instead of a replacement?

Yes, absolutely. For valid claims within the guarantee period, you can choose either a replacement bird or a full refund. It's entirely your decision.

What if the bird gets sick after 30 days?

The 30-day health guarantee covers pre-existing conditions and genetic issues. If a health issue appears after 30 days, it's likely environmental or acquired. However, we still provide free consultation and advice for the lifetime of the bird.

Do I need to use a specific veterinarian?

No, you can use any licensed veterinarian. We just need the official diagnosis and documentation for health-related claims.

What if I don't follow the quarantine protocol?

The health guarantee becomes void if you don't quarantine new birds. Mixing new birds with existing flock immediately exposes them to new pathogens, making it impossible to determine the source of any illness.

Does the guarantee transfer if I sell the bird?

No, the guarantee is non-transferable and only valid for the original purchaser. This ensures we can track care and maintain our quality standards.

What about breeding performance?

We guarantee breeding-age birds will be capable of reproduction. If a bird proves infertile or has breeding issues due to genetic causes, it's covered under our 30-day guarantee.

How long do claims take to process?

We review all claims within 24 hours and resolve valid claims within 48 hours. Our goal is to make the process as fast and painless as possible.
