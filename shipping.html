Frequently Asked Questions

Can I track my shipment?

Yes! You'll receive GPS tracking information and carrier contact details. You can follow your birds' journey in real-time and contact the carrier directly with any questions.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

You MUST be present to receive live birds. We confirm delivery windows 24 hours in advance. If you cannot be available, we can reschedule or hold birds at a climate-controlled facility (additional fees may apply).

Do you ship during winter?

Yes, we ship year-round using climate-controlled transport. However, we may delay shipments during extreme weather events for bird safety.

Can I choose my carrier?

We work with trusted, licensed livestock transporters who specialize in poultry. While we select the carrier based on route and availability, you're welcome to discuss preferences.

What happens if a bird dies during shipping?

In our 37+ years, this has happened less than 2% of the time. If it occurs, we replace the bird immediately at no cost under our live arrival guarantee.

Can I combine shipping with another order?

Absolutely! If you're ordering multiple birds over time, we can coordinate a single shipment. Contact us to arrange timing.

Do you handle international customs and permits?

Yes! We have extensive experience with international livestock shipping. We handle all USDA export documentation, health certificates, and coordinate with customs brokers in your country. You'll receive guidance on any destination-specific requirements.

What are the quarantine requirements for international shipping?

Quarantine requirements vary by country. Some require pre-shipment quarantine in the US, others require quarantine upon arrival. We provide detailed information specific to your country and assist with all compliance requirements.

How long does international shipping take?

International shipments typically take 3-7 days depending on destination and customs clearance. This includes transit time and any required quarantine periods. We provide detailed timelines during the order process.
